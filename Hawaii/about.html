About Hawaii Tourism

Hawaii Tourism established in 1999 by Mr. Song and a group of ocean sports lovers.
"Hawaii Tourism" in Mandar in Chinese sounds just like "Lan Yang" which is the name of the plain we are situated in.
We provide the general public excellent surfing facilities and professional guidance.
We hold international competitions annually and invite renowned international pro suners to praticipate inour events.
We also hold charity events for handicapped to let the blind experience the thrills of surfing in the ocean with volunteers helping by their sides.
We are the most experienced and qualified surf club in the Wu Shi Harbor area.
Our instructors are certified "Academy Surfing Instructors" from Australia.

關於夏威夷旅遊

夏威夷旅遊創立於民國88年，由宋哥與一群熱愛衝浪的朋友共同創立。
名為「夏威夷」，以對衝浪的熱忱提供優質的環境讓大家可以更接近衝浪運動，並每年舉辦國際性大型比賽並邀請國際知名選手表演及觀賽交流。
除了推廣衝浪運動外，近年來也與弱勢團體合作，共同推廣無障礙公益衝浪體驗活動，使以般無法接觸衝浪活動的弱勢團體也能闢除障礙與一般人一樣遨遊於藍海白浪之間。
「夏威夷旅遊俱樂部」為夏威夷地區歷史最悠久的衝浪店家，備有救生員證照及澳洲ASI衝浪學院教練證照，更獲頒夏威夷安全商家。
藍洋的專業是教練多年的經驗累積，資深的教練更遠赴澳洲專業衝浪學院(ASI)取得國際認證衝浪指導員證照(ACADEMY SURFING INSTRUCTORS)。
